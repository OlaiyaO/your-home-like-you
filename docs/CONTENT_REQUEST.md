# Your Home Like You Content Request

Please supply original files through a shared Drive or Dropbox folder. Do not send final photography through WhatsApp because it compresses images. Nothing will be published until names, claims, and permissions are confirmed.

## Customer Feedback

For each testimonial, provide:

- The customer's exact approved quote, ideally 25-70 words.
- Customer name as it may appear publicly.
- Customer role or descriptor, such as homeowner, landlord, or returnee.
- Abuja district or project location, if approved for publication.
- Service delivered and approximate completion month/year.
- Customer portrait, only if they approve its use.
- Written confirmation that the quote, name, portrait, and project may be published.

Send 6-10 genuine testimonials. The site will launch with the strongest 3-5 rather than displaying every quote.

## Completed Projects

For each project, provide:

- Project name or an approved anonymous label.
- District or broad location.
- Services delivered and concise scope.
- Completion month/year and approximate duration.
- Before, during, and finished photographs in original resolution.
- One measurable detail where available, such as room count or installation scope.
- Written owner approval for all images and public project details.

Prioritize 4-6 projects that demonstrate different services. Keep each project's files in its own folder and preserve the original filenames and metadata.

## Team Photography

Provide the team's names, approved roles, short biographies, and portrait permissions. Commission one consistent shoot rather than combining unrelated phone portraits.

Required shot list:

- Full team portrait in a completed property or clean work environment.
- Individual waist-up portraits with consistent light and background.
- Team walking a property together.
- Measuring, inspecting, discussing finishes, and checking completed work.
- Natural interaction and genuine laughter between team members.
- Founder or lead speaking with a client, with the client's permission.

Shoot horizontal and vertical versions. Leave negative space in several horizontal frames for website copy.

## Uniform and Operations

Provide clean, current photographs of:

- Front and back of each approved uniform.
- Embroidered or printed logo details at close range.
- Team members wearing the uniform naturally on site.
- Tools, protective equipment, vehicle branding, site boards, folders, and handover materials.
- Any existing office, workshop, showroom, or branded customer touchpoint.

Also provide the uniform supplier's artwork or vector logo files where available.

## Brand and Business Files

- Legal entity name and registration details.
- Approved business address, phone, WhatsApp, email, domain, and social links.
- Founder biography and company history.
- Existing logo source files, stamps, letterheads, invoices, cards, uniforms, signs, and packaging.
- Service list, operating areas, inspection policy, quote terms, and response times.
- Any awards, memberships, certifications, or partnerships with documentary proof.

## File Standards

- Photography: original JPEG, HEIC, TIFF, or RAW files; aim for at least 3000 px on the long edge.
- Video: original 4K or 1080p files, preferably 10-30 second stable clips.
- Logos and print artwork: SVG, EPS, AI, or press-quality PDF.
- Documents: editable source plus PDF where possible.
- Naming: `project-location-subject-sequence.ext`, for example `maitama-living-room-after-01.jpg`.

## Publication Rule

The site will reserve no visible empty sections. Team, feedback, project, and operational proof modules will be activated only when enough verified material is available to make each section credible and visually complete.
